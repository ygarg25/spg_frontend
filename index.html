In-Depth Process Knowledge

We have more than 15 years of experience in providing data entry services to multiple companies across India. We process more than 1 lakh data entry applications annually for multiple clients across financial investment and insurance space. Through this, we have obtained considerable process knowledge and expertise on all aspects concerning the data entry process. Our firm is assisted by a team of 10+ year experienced Chartered Accountants and Engineers.

Data Collection and KYC Centre

Apart from providing online data entry services, we also provide physical data collection and KYC centre where end customer can submit data and KYC forms. Our office is located centrally in Ambala city and have proper arrangement for ensuring great customer experience.

Strict Quality Accuracy Compliance

We maintain strict control and processes to ensure that accuracy of the inputted data. We serve multiple clients with a high quality data entry requirement and ensure a time-bound high quality delivery.

Large Volume Handling Capability

We have a vast experience of handling large volume of data entry for 10+ years. We serve some of the biggest corporate clients and provide them high quality & time bound delivery.

Mutual fund application processing

The firm is working on behalf of CAMS (Computer Age Management Services). We do processing of mutual funds investment applications on behalf of 18+ mutual fund companies. We process roughly 1 lakh applications annually from mutual fund investors of Ambala District. We process client's KYC, application for new investment purchase and redemptions.

Life insurance application processing

The firm process the life insurance applications on behalf of TATA AIG. We process client's KYC and application for new insurance purchase. We also manage collection of the periodical insurance premiums (in cash and cheques) from clients.

Auto loan application processing

The firm handles loan applications on behalf of Hero Fincorp which is engaged in financing of Hero Honda 2 & 3 wheelers. We work with numerous local Hero Honda auto dealers and process the loan financing application for new auto purchasers. We process roughly 10000+ auto loan applications annually.

About us
